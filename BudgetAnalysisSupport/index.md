## Kontakt

Har du spørsmål, funnet en feil eller har et forslag?

## Vanlige spørsmål

**Hvor lagres dataene mine?**
Alt lagres lokalt på enheten din. Ingen konto, ingen server.
Se [personvernerklæringen](../privacy.html) for detaljer.

**Hvordan sletter jeg alt?**
Slett enkeltdata i appen, eller slett appen for å fjerne alt.

**Hvordan sier jeg opp Premium?**
Abonnementet administreres av App Store: Innstillinger → navnet ditt → Abonnementer.

# Support – Budget Analysis (English)

## Contact

Questions, bugs or suggestions?
Email **adrianjahnsrud@live.no** and I'll get back to you as soon as I can.

## FAQ

**Where is my data stored?**
Everything is stored locally on your device. No account, no server.
See the [privacy policy](../privacy.html#english) for details.

**How do I delete everything?**
Delete individual items in the app, or delete the app to remove all data.

**How do I cancel Premium?**
The subscription is managed by the App Store: Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
